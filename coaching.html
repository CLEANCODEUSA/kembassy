It’s Okay to Be Different

With Jacynth’s coaching you will get clarity and conviction in what matters for you.

You will get curious about the challenges you are facing and create new directions and beliefs in order to overcome these. We will work together and you will ultimately decide your own happiness

This Coaching is for you.

  • If you are ready to look for the answers, you’re ready for an insight into how life works.
  • In spite of your success the fear of being different is preventing you from trying new things, meeting new people, missing potential
    opportunities
  • A sudden or unexpected change in your life has left you feeling shaken, a little unsure and questioning the essence of who you are
  • If you are sick and tired of the challenging thoughts and emotions that are taking up space, and you just want life to be easier
  • You are not looking for someone to fix you or do the work for you.
  • You are ready to reignite your passion, fulfil your dreams and acknowledge your brilliance.

My Transformation Coaching Packages

Sapphire 1

Your package includes

  • One to One Coaching
  • Eight Months
  • 7 steps signature package
  • One 60 minute session every two weeks (total of 16 hours)
  • One hour bonus session (free)
  • Email support between scheduled sessions
  • Each session we co-create and agree actions for you
  • Weekly email check in support
  • Free accountability call 2 months after last session
  • POA

The Diamond package will provide you with support for eight months and will enable you to realise your truly authentic, confident and effective self, Acknowledging Your Brilliance.

Jacynth truly hears what is needed, her coaching offers a full transformation in thinking and actions towards your longer-term goals, it is for those seeking true authenticity and change in their life.

This is a comprehensive package to assist you with addressing current life matters as well as seeing your hopes and dreams unfold. This package will provide you with a sound platform for transforming, enriching and being in the present.

By the end of eight months you will notice a significant and profound effect from the changes that you have made and acknowledge “It’s okay to be different”

Are you ready to move to the next level? Jacynth is the transformational coach you need on your side. You can trust Jacynth to guide you toward the future you deserve. Take action TODAY!
